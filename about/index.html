About me and the blog

I am a postdoctoral researcher focusing on superconducting quantum computing chip fabrication. Especially for the fabrication process integration and the project managing/integration to accelerate the turn over time.
In my PhD, I studied classical 2+1D nonlinear waveform evolution in dust acoustic wave turbulence.

In this blog, I share my learning notes that I learnt from the research and daily life.

Interests

Research/Technology

  • Superconducting qubit chip fabrication
  • Integration of semiconductor fabrication process (metal deposition, dielectric deposition, etching)
  • Failure analysis of the fabrication (including SEM/TEM/AFM)
  • Spatiotemporal evolution of the nonlinear waveform evolution in weakly disordered dust acoustic wave

Business Interests

  • Silicon photonics
  • Development of quantum computing hardware and software

Engineering & Programming

  • Process integration and failure analysis (including SEM/TEM image analysis, resistance analysis)
  • Linux/Fedora(RHEL system)
